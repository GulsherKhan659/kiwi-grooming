About Kiwi Grooming

Crafting Confidence Since 1990

Welcome to Kiwi Grooming Where Precision Meets Personality

Sanctuary of Style Since 1990

Founded as a neighborhood cornerstone, Kiwi Grooming has evolved into a modern grooming destination while keeping old-world craftsmanship alive. Our licensed master barbers blend time-honored techniques with contemporary trends, using only premium products to transform haircuts into confidence-building experiences.

More than a barbershop – we’re curators of self-expression. From sharp fades to tailored beard sculpting, every service is a collaboration. Our leather-chair conversations and whisky-barrel décor invite you to unwind while we refine your signature look. This is where generations of men have discovered their edge.

Experience the Kiwi Standard: Meticulous attention, spotless tools, and results that turn heads for weeks. Your throne awaits.
